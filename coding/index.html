What is Physics?


1. Physics, science that deals with the structure of matter and the interactions between the fundamental constituents of the observable universe.

2. Its scope of study encompasses not only the behaviour of objects under the action of given forces but also the nature and origin of gravitational, electromagnetic, and nuclear force fields.

3. Physics can, at base, be defined as the science of matter, motion, and energy.

4. Its laws are typically expressed with economy and precision in the language of mathematics.

5. Everything happens due to Physics
